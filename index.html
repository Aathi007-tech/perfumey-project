About us

Step into a world of timeless elegance with Ethereal Bloom, a fragrance that embodies grace, sensuality, and pure sophistication. Opening with the radiant freshness of bergamot and pear, this scent immediately captivates the senses, offering a luminous and uplifting start. As the fragrance unfolds, a delicate heart of jasmine, rose, and peony blooms, creating a soft yet intoxicating floral embrace. These exquisite notes intertwine seamlessly, evoking the charm of a sunlit garden in full bloom.
